This mod that adds the bullwhip from the Indiana Jones movie franchises.

**Why I decided to make this mod**

I like exploring and combat in Minecraft, its my faverote things to do in the game. But every once in a while I think it can get boring, just hitting mobs and with no special finisheres or cool take downs with a weapon. So thats why I made this mod!

It also works with my other mod [Better Fist Fighting](https://github.com/Dozzidog/Better-Fist-Fighting)!
