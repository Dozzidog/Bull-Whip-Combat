When I watch the first Indiana Jones movie Raiders of the Lost Ark as a kid I IMMEDIENTLY fell in love with the franchise. I had both LEGO: Indiana Jones games on our family WII U before we got rid of it and the first three Indiana Jones movies, Indiana Jones and the Emperor's Tomb on our Family Xbox One and got rid of the Xbox as well, and Indiana Jones and the Great Circle on my personal PC and got it as soon as it officaly released.

I do underestand that Minecraft isn't supposed to be that way and thats fine thats why we have Forge, Fabric etc. to mod the game as we like to, and I LOVED the idea of the wbullwhip from the Indiana Jones movies or game to be in Minecraft I think that would make exploration and combat more fun but never found a mod that added it into the game.
