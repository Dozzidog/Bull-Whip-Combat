This mod that adds the bullwhip from the Indiana Jones movie franchises.

**Peronal backstory and how I decided to come up with this mod**

When I watch the first Indiana Jones movie Raiders of the Lost Ark as a kid I IMMEDIENTLY fell in love with the franchise. I had both LEGO: Indiana Jones games on our family WII U before we got rid of it and the first three Indiana Jones movies, Indiana Jones and the Emperor's Tomb on our Family Xbox One and got rid of the Xbox as well, and Indiana Jones and the Great Circle on my personal PC and got it as soon as it officaly released.

**Why I decided to come up with this mod**

I like exploring and combat in Minecraft, its my faverote things to do in the game. But every once in a while I think it can get boring just hitting mobs while waiting on a cooldown and with no special finisheres or cool take downs. I do underestand that Minecraft isn't supposed to be that way and thats fine thats why we have mod loaders like Forge, Fabric, NeoForge, and others to mod the game, and I LOVED the idea of the bullwhip from the Indiana Jones movies and games to be in Minecraft I think that would make exploration and combat more fun but never found a mod that added it into the game. So I decided to create one myself!
